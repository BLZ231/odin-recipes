Calzone Recipe

Description

Savory and cheesy, this four cheese calzone is the ultimate cheese lovers dream! A blend of creamy mozzarella and fontina, sharp pecorino cheese and delicate ricotta are layered inside a perfectly golden pizza crust.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Olive Oil
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Fontina Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pecorino Cheese

Steps

  1. Roll the pizza dough out to a 9 inch circle.
  2. Layer the four cheeses in the center.
  3. Fold the dough over and make sure the calzone is completely sealed.
  4. Brush a thin layer of olive oil on top of the calzone.
  5. Cut two small slits on top of the dough.
  6. Bake the calzone in a preheated oven at 500 degrees for 8 minutes, or until the dough is gold and crisp.
