Honey Truffle Recipe

Description

Honestly I don't know what to put here. I vaguely remember liking these as a kid, so I tried googling for the recipe and was surprised that I was able to find it. Kind of obscure, but I figured it would work for this

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Milk chocolate morsels
  • 1/4 cup sweet cocoa

Steps

  1. Make sure the peanut butter and honey are at room temperature.
  2. Mix the peanut butter, honey, and vanilla extract until all the ingredients are blended smooth.
  3. Continue to mix the ingredients while gradually adding the confectioner's sugar a bit at a time until it's all blended smooth.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes or until the dough has slightly stiffened.
  5. Place the sweetened cocoa in another small bowl.
  6. Use a teaspoon to scoop out a bit of dough, place 2 or 3 milk chocolate morsels in the center, and roll it into a ball.
  7. Drop the ball of dough into the bowl of cocoa and roll it around until it is completely coated.
  8. Repeat the previous two steps until all the dough has been used up.
  9. Place the truffles on a plate and keep them in the refrigerator for 2 hours or until the truffles have slightly hardened.
